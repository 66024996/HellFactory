How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your personal data associated with our app, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook account settings.
  2. Find our app in the list of connected apps and remove it.
  3. Send an email to support@example.com requesting the deletion of your data, and include your Facebook User ID.

Once we receive your request, we will delete your data within 7 days.
